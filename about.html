A brief history about us

Founded in 2006, Percival Logistics has grown from a small local courier service to a global logistics provider. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has been the cornerstone of our success. Over the years, we have expanded our services and established a strong presence in key markets around the world.

Our mission is to provide reliable and efficient logistics solutions that help our clients succeed in their businesses. We pride ourselves on our innovative approach, leveraging technology to optimize our operations and deliver superior service.

At Percival Logistics, we are dedicated to building long-term partnerships with our clients, understanding their unique needs, and providing tailored solutions that drive their success. We look forward to continuing our journey of growth and excellence in the logistics industry.
